## Response

All fixes applied. Here's the full summary:

## Root Cause

The app name uses a specific casing convention: **dockyard.AI** (lowercase "d", uppercase "AI"). Several locations in the codebase were using the incorrect casing **Dockyard.ai** (uppercase "D", lowercase "ai").

## Changes Made

| File | Line(s) | Before | After |
|---|---|---|---|
| `web/src/App.tsx` | 405 | `<h1>Dockyard.ai</h1>` | `<h1>dockyard.AI</h1>` |
| `web/src/App.tsx` | 417 | `placeholder="Ask Dockyard.ai to do something..."` | `placeholder="Ask dockyard.AI to do something..."` |
| `web/src/App.tsx` | 429 | `confirm('Sign out of Dockyard.ai?')` | `confirm('Sign out of dockyard.AI?')` |
| `web/index.html` | 9 | `<title>Dockyard.ai</title>` | `<title>dockyard.AI</title>` |
| `web/public/manifest.json` | 2 | `"name": "Dockyard.ai"` | `"name": "dockyard.AI"` |

The primary fix (per the issue) is the toolbar `<h1>` brand heading on line 405 of `App.tsx`. The other locations were updated for consistency since they all display the same app name.
